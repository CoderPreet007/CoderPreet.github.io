About Me

Data Analyst!

Welcome to my Data Analysis portfolio website.

I am a passionate data analyst with a strong background in Mathematics.

With a love for numbers and an eye for detail, I thrive on discovering insights from complex data sets that help drive business decisions.

As a data analyst, I specialize in Financial Analysis, using tools such as SQL, Python, Excel, Tableau, Power BI to turn raw data into meaningful insights. I am well-versed in data visualization, data cleaning, and data modeling, and I am always seeking to expand my skillset.

During my studies, I gained hands-on experience working on projects that involved analyzing data, which allowed me to apply my knowledge in a real-world setting.

Aside from my technical skills, I am also an effective communicator, able to explain complex data analysis concepts to both technical and non-technical audiences. I am a team player and thrive in collaborative environments, where I can learn from my colleagues while contributing my expertise.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio website, and I hope you enjoy exploring my work. If you have any questions or would like to discuss potential projects, please do not hesitate to reach out.
